My Experience
Country Rank Description Food Entertainment
Singapore 10/10 Sinagapore is a wealthy city/country located in Southeast Asia. During my visit I noticed outstanding malls for shopping and entertainment. It had amazing night life and many things to do for tourists. Although the city itself was amazing all the citizens kept to themselves for the most part. The food in Singapore was incredible, it made it hard to go back to food in the US. In Singapore we spent our time walking through the massive malls, sightseeing or staying out late for the night life
Malaysia 8/10 The part of Malyasia we stayed in felt like a rainforest, we hiked through mountains and went swimming with the locals. Everyone in Malaysia was extremely nice and outgoing making it such a fun place to visit. Some of the food we ate in Malaysia I had never before seen or even heard of, it was fun to try the local fruit and new foods. In Malaysia hiking and swimming and immersing ourselves in the nature was what we did with most our time there.
South Africa 10/10 South Africa offers unforgettable wildlife experiences. From safaris to stunning beaches, it's a diverse destination and many say a South African safari is a necessary experience in a lifetime. Indulging in diverse cuisine influenced by various cultures. Embarking on safaris, exploring beaches and cities.
France 8/10 France is rich in history and culture. Paris's architecture is stunning, and the museums offer incredible insights. There were many fancy restaurants in France with great food. In France we explored beautiful historical landmarks and architecture and going to museums such as the Louvre.
Germany 9/10 Germany offers a blend of modernity and history. Cities like Munich boast unique architecture and rich history. When touring the country we learned much about WWII making it a very meaningful trip. The food in Germany was amazing and unique. Exploring historical sites like Dachau and Munich.
United Kingdom 7/10 The UK is a mix of history, culture, and natural beauty. Some of the highlights of my trip were learning about the history of the monarchy and seeing the architecture of the city of London. The food was pretty similar to the US Spent most of our time sightseeing and learning about the history

If one of your favorite pastimes is walking around malls and shopping, Singapore could be the perfect destination for your trip. You can get lost in the multiple story malls that stretched for miles and were at every corner of the city. Areas of the mall specifically for luxury and expensive brands and others for entertainment and more fun.

If you enjoy traveling to learn about the history of the countries you are in, France is your ultimate destination. Museums such as the Louvre display art from hundreds of years ago such as the Mona Lisa. Architecture in this country has been standing since the middle ages. Even if you aren't a history fanatic, you can still enjoy this old country.

For someone who enjoys wildlife and wants to experience some of the most majestic animals on Earth, South Africa is the perfect destination. Furthermore, South Africa offers beautiful beaches on the Indian Ocean and cities such as Cape Town.
