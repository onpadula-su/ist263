Summer of 2024 I flew to South Africa for a 9 day trip. There is nowhere else on earth where you can witness these animals as upclose and personal as on a South African safari.

I was only able to spend a day in Paris but the architecture is beautiful. There is so much history to take in through museums but next we went to the city of Normandy to learn about the history from WWII.

In summer of 2024, my father and I traveled to Germany. He always had an interest for WWII so we visited Dachau and Munich to learn about the history. The unique architecture was amazing and the history is incredible.

The first time I had ever been to Europe I stayed in London. We learned alot about the government and culture surrounding the Queen. I think the European architecture is much more impressive than our cities in the US.

I went to Malaysia with three of my best friends after my junior year of high school. The people there were so welcoming and they really made our trip as fun as it was. The climate is very unique, feeling like we were living in a rainforest. It was fun trying the local fruits and foods I had never before seen.

Singapore is definitely one of the my favorite places I have ever been to. There is so much shopping and malls with so much to do. The night life was incredible, the food was incredible, and the city itself is beautiful.

If one of your favorite pastimes is walking around malls and shopping, Singapore could be the perfect destination for your trip. You can get lost in the multiple story malls that stretched for miles and were at every corner of the city. Areas of the mall specifically for luxury and expensive brands and others for entertainment and more fun.

If you enjoy traveling to learn about the history of the countries you are in, France is your ultimate destination. Museums such as the Louvre display art from hundreds of years ago such as the Mona Lisa. Architecture in this country has been standing since the middle ages. Even if you aren't a history fanatic, you can still enjoy this old country.

For someone who enjoys wildlife and wants to experience some of the most majestic animals on Earth, South Africa is the perfect destination. Furthermore, South Africa offers beautiful beaches on the Indian Ocean and cities such as Cape Town.
